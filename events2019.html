An IEEE Seminar has been organized by IEEE IAS SB Chapter of IIT Mandi in association with IEEE PES-IAS and PELS-IES Chapters
Venue: A4 Conference Room

Workshop: FPGA based Systems Synthesis by Xilinx

A one-day workshop was been organized by IEEE IAS SB Chapter of IIT Mandi in association with IEEE PES- IAS Chapter, Delhi Section. The workshop was on "FPGA based Systems Synthesis" conducted by Xilinx.

Meeting: IEEE IAS SB Chapter EXECOM Meeting

3rd Execom Meeting of IEEE IAS SB Chapter IIT Mandi was scheduled with agenda's:
1. Induction of new IEEE IAS Student Members.
2. Follow up action on the minutes of last Execom meeting. 
3. Welcome of New IEEE Robotics Society and introduction to new members to the existing IAS society to the newly formed Robotics Society.
4. Formation of New Execom Members.
5. Filing of Petition to open IEEE Women in Engineering Society of IIT Mandi
6. Plan of activities for the year 2019.

Date: 5th March 2019.
